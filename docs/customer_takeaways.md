# TodayBank GRC on Databricks – One-Page Leave-Behind

## What you saw today

A unified Governance, Risk, and Compliance platform on Databricks that handles AML monitoring, fair-lending review, customer-complaint analytics, KYC document automation, and model risk management – on **one** governance model, **one** data layer, **one** audit trail.

## The five pieces that make it work

1. **Unity Catalog** – One governance umbrella over tables, views, files, models, dashboards, and AI agents. Tags, lineage, and audit are automatic.
2. **Lakeflow Pipelines** – Declarative ETL with built-in data quality expectations and schema enforcement. Replaces SSIS / Informatica / Glue.
3. **AI Functions** (`ai_classify`, `ai_extract`, `ai_query`) – LLM capabilities as SQL primitives. Governed and audited like any query.
4. **MLflow + Lakehouse Monitoring** – Model registry, SR 11-7 documentation, drift detection. MRM-ready out of the box.
5. **AI/BI Dashboards + Genie** – Curated dashboards for risk officers, natural-language self-service for analysts, all with the SQL shown.
